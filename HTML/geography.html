Geography

  Portsmouth, UK
Weather Info Over 10 Days

Monday

On Monday the 11th of March we will be experiencing quite a cloudy day! With highs of 10 degrees Celsius or 50 for my fellow Fahrenheit folks (we love America), and lows of 7 degrees Celsius or again 45 for my Fahrenheit folks you are going to need your extra fluffy coats to keep warm as there will also be some North-West 8.9-mile winds! Humidity levels will be around 87% and rainfall will be about 0.3mm. Now that's out the way, let's move on to Tuesday.

Tuesday

On Tuesday we will be having, yet another cloudy day. We will be having highs of 14 degrees Celsius (57 degrees Fahrenheit) and lows of 6 degrees Celsius (43 degrees Fahrenheit) (how chilly!). However, you may need to consider getting an umbrella and a coat as we will be having 8.2mm of precipitation, a strong west 14-mile wind, and humidity levels of 92%!

Wednesday

Moving on to Wednesday we will be having a cloudy, yet sunny day. Highs will reach 13°C (55°F) and lows will dip down to 8°C (46°F), so it's a day for a light jacket. Unlike the wet Tuesday though, Wednesday looks to be drier with little to no precipitation. Humidity levels will also be lower compared to Tuesday, sitting at around 86%.

Thursday

On Thursday it is going to be raining, again! Highs will go up to 12 degrees Celsius and lows will dip down to 11 degrees Celsius. We will be having 2.4mm of precipitation, a 14 mile per hour northeast wind and 86% humidity.

Friday

For Friday we will have a relatively sunny day! With highs of 13 degrees and lows of 6 degrees a good old coat will suffice. Wind will be coming our way from the North-East at an average of 20 miles per hour. With a slight drizzle of only 0.7mm of rain and 85% humidity, you are in for a nice day! Now our week is done, let’s check out our weekend!

Saturday

On Saturday we will be having some warm sunshine in the mornings, while the rest of the day it will be raining with 0.4mm of precipitation and 79% humidity. The temperatures will be pretty much the same as the whole week. With highs of 12 degrees and lows of 10 degrees, temperatures will remain stable and nice. Not to mention, winds are going to be gentler, coming from the North at around 8 miles per hour. Since Saturday is done, let's check out Sunday! Surely it will be warmer!

Sunday

On Sunday we will be having heavy clouds and 95% humidity. The hottest temperature that we will have is 12 degrees meanwhile the lowest temperature will be 10 degrees. Precipitation wise- we will be having around 5.4mm of rain. In terms of wind, we will be having relatively normal winds, coming from the North-East at around 10 miles per hour.

Monday

Finishing the weekend on a relatively wet note, let’s hope that our remaining three days will look a little nicer. Monday, more rain! We will have 5.2mm of rain and 87% humidity in our atmosphere. The hottest we will get on Monday will be 12 degrees, while the coldest it’s going to get it around 8 degrees. And not to mention, we will also be getting some gentle winds from the North, coming in at around 7 miles per hour.

Tuesday

On Tuesday we will some sun with some cloudy times. Humidity levels will be around 93%, due to the rain that will be happening on Monday. The highest temperature will be 11 degrees and the lowest temperature will be 9 degrees. There will be 0 to no precipitation. This would be a nice day to go on a walk! Also, some gentle winds coming from the North at 10 miles per hour mean that it shouldn’t get too cold.

Wednesday

And finally, Wednesday! Let’s see what Wednesday has to offer. Wednesday, finally a sunny day! All day we will have a bright sun with highs of 11 degrees and lows of 8 degrees. Humidity levels will be about 91% and there will be 0.1mm of precipitation. The wind will be relatively the same as Tuesday with most of the wind coming from the North at 10 miles per hour.
